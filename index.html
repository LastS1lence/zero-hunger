Our Project

Introduction

Hunger is important because food is necessary for people to live a healthy life. Hunger affects poor families, children, and people who cannot afford enough food. We learned about hunger by researching online and talking with our teacher about how many people in the world do not have enough food.

50 things gave

Planning

We planned to go and give food to poor families to help them make some savings or invest elsewhere, rather than in food. But it was very hard to find a date and a place.

+7 bénéficiaires

Action

Finally, we found at Kaciru 3 families in need, so we gave them chicken, oil, rice, vegetables, and fruits.

+7 bénéficiaires

Reflection

We did a good job, but we need to be more concentrated and set a fixed date that the whole group is okay with, and not do it on the last day that we can. At least we did it and finished everything on time and more importantly the families where happy.
